The Vision

We decided to tackle the modern day challenge of finding movies to watch while you patiently wait for the world to return to normal. We scoured the internet looking for ideas on how to customize movie recommendations to each individual. We looked at multiple websites such as Netflix, Hulu, HBO, Vudu, etc., to help create the most optimized recommendation engine available.

The Path

To begin we needed to find a database for all the information we need to gather. We started with TMDB. A movie website that hosts large amounts of data on movies, tv, and actors. We also pulled data from IMDB to help expand the information our system can utilize.

Once the data was called and cleaned to only hold the information we needed, we began to split up the tasks needed to create a functioning website. We divided the tasks into a few main sections, the search engine, the recommendation engine, the website, and the presentation.

It is our hope that you find great pleasure and ease when using this website! We hope that the recommendations provided help create wonderful memories either by yourself or with your friends and family!
